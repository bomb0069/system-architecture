X-Axis — Schema Flexibility (Rigid ↔ Flexible)
Rigid: Schema defined upfront and enforced on every write. Enables constraints, foreign keys, and optimizer efficiency — but migrations are costly.
Flexible: Each record can have different fields. Enables rapid evolution — but sacrifices enforcement and query predictability.
Y-Axis — Data Connectivity (Connected ↔ Flat)
Connected: Data entities are meaningfully linked — queries must traverse relationships. The relationship IS the query.
Flat / Sequential: Records are independent or time-ordered. Optimized for key lookups or time-range scans.
