Y-Axis — Control (High ↔ Low)
High Control (top): You configure OS, network, storage, replication — everything. You also own every failure and patch cycle.
Low Control (bottom): Vendor abstracts all infrastructure. Faster to deploy, harder to customize deeply.
X-Axis — Cost at Scale (Low ↔ High)
Low Cost (left): Open-source tooling or simple compute pricing. Scales cheaply if your team can manage the ops burden.
High Cost (right): Premium managed services, enterprise licensing, or hyperscaler pricing that compounds with every feature you add.
