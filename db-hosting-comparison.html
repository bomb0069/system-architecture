Y-Axis — Operational Control (High ↔ Low)
High Control (top): You own the infrastructure. You configure everything — replication, tuning, security, networking. You also own every failure.
Low Control (bottom): Vendor manages patching, backups, failover, and scaling. You configure only what the vendor exposes. Faster to start, harder to customize.
X-Axis — Cost at Scale (Low ↔ High)
Low Cost (left): Open-source software or pay-only-for-compute models. Scales cheaply if you manage it yourself or choose usage-based pricing wisely.
High Cost (right): Enterprise licensing, per-seat fees, or premium managed services. Cost grows faster than workload — but so does support and compliance coverage.
