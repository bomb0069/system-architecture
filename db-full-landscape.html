X-Axis — Schema Flexibility (Rigid ↔ Flexible)
Rigid: Schema defined upfront, enforced on every write. Enables constraints, foreign keys, and query optimization — but migrations are costly.
Flexible: Each record can have different fields. Enables rapid evolution and polymorphic data — but sacrifices enforcement and predictability.
Y-Axis — Data Connectivity (Connected ↔ Flat)
Connected: Data entities are meaningfully linked — queries must traverse or join relationships. Relationship IS the query.
Flat / Sequential: Records are largely independent or ordered by time. No traversal needed — optimized for key lookups or time-range scans.
